About The Project

The online Food Museum of Asia is a unique platform that invites visitors to explore the rich culinary heritage of the continent. The website highlights the vibrant diversity of Asian cuisines, uncovering the unique ingredients and shared traditions that shape them. Through this journey, the museum delves into the cultural and historical significance of food, offering insights into the everyday lives, traditions, and values of different nations. By showcasing both their similarities and differences, it helps visitors better understand and appreciate diverse cultures and lifestyles of Asia.

Map of Museum

The map section helps visitors understand the interconnectivity and relationships between items within each narrative, showcasing how dishes, ingredients, and traditions are linked across regions. By presenting detailed maps, the website reveals the origins of each dish and illustrates how they relate to one another, offering a deeper understanding of the cultural and historical connections in the physical exhibition.
